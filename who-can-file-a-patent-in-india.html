Who can file a patent in India?

One of the most frequently asked questions regarding patents is, who can file it? In India, business owners with a new idea or products can file patents. Scientists with a new formula or concept to patent. Professionals with new software or business method. Masters or Ph.D. holders with a research project to patent. Students with intent to learn more about patents and how they can protect their ideas can file patents in India.
